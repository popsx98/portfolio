Projects

Selected evaluations and research projects across business & skills, youth/community, and environment/heritage — with an emphasis on practical, decision-ready outputs.

Business & Skills Support — Programme Evaluation

Led an end-to-end evaluation covering SMEs and training providers. Built a theory of change and proportionate measurement framework, ran interviews/focus groups/surveys, and triangulated findings to show what worked, for whom, and why.

  • Mixed Methods
  • SME & Provider Stakeholders
  • Recommendations & KPIs

Outcome: prioritised changes to outreach and course design; created a lean KPI set for ongoing monitoring.

Youth & Community Initiatives — Primary Research

Delivered sensitive primary research with young people and vulnerable groups using trauma-informed practice and ethical safeguards. Co-designed engagement to reduce burden, and produced accessible summaries for practitioners and funders.

  • TIP & Ethics
  • Qualitative Depth
  • Accessible Outputs

Outcome: partners adjusted delivery models and safeguarding workflows based on lived-experience feedback.

Environment & Heritage — Evaluation Frameworks

Developed logic models, indicators and data plans for sustainability, property/land and heritage programmes, aligned to Magenta Book standards. Clarified feasible evidence pathways where full counterfactuals weren’t proportionate.

  • ToC & Logic Models
  • Magenta Book
  • Proportionate Evidence

Outcome: funders adopted right-sized monitoring and clearer success criteria for future bids.

Data Visual Storytelling — Exec Summaries & Dashboards

Produced concise, visual executive summaries and infographics to communicate key results at-a-glance; supported simple Power BI dashboards where appropriate.

  • Infogram
  • Piktochart
  • Power BI (light)

Outcome: decision-makers could scan insights quickly; reports gained better engagement.

Internal Brand Refresh — Visuals Hub & Templates

Standardised colour system, slide/report templates and iconography. Set up a Visuals Hub to speed up production and improve quality across teams.

  • Design System
  • Templates
  • Ops Enablement

Outcome: faster report turnaround and more consistent client-facing materials.
