Profile

I specialise in monitoring and evaluation and mixed-methods research, with a focus on practical evidence that informs decisions and improves delivery.

Skills

Monitoring & Evaluation

  • Designs practical M&E frameworks (theory of change, logic models, indicators) aligned to Magenta Book good practice.
  • Process and impact evaluations; contribution analysis and proportionate approaches for smaller programmes.
  • Outcome mapping/harvesting for complex, multi-partner initiatives.

Research & Analysis

  • Mixed-methods primary research: interviews, focus groups, online surveys, observational work.
  • Data synthesis across qual + quant with clear evidence trails; experienced with NVivo/QDA Miner and Excel.
  • Ethical, trauma-informed practice when working with young people and vulnerable groups.

Data Visual Storytelling

  • Executive summaries, infographics, and deck narratives that surface “so what” insights.
  • Infogram / Piktochart for reports; familiarity with Power BI for simple dashboards.
  • Templates and design systems to standardise outputs across teams.

Consulting & Delivery

  • Client management, scoping, bid writing, and stakeholder engagement across public, charity, and SME contexts.
  • Mentoring juniors and onboarding; supports recruitment and internal quality processes.
  • Clear report writing for decision-makers; confident workshops and presentations.

Tools & Web

  • Microsoft 365 (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Teams, SharePoint); SurveyMonkey, Snap, Forms, Mentimeter.
  • HTML & CSS — self-taught, used to prototype pages and present portfolios (side skill).

Experience

I plan and deliver mixed-methods evaluation and research projects end-to-end — from framing theories of change and proportionate logic models, through primary research with stakeholders (interviews, focus groups and e-surveys), to synthesis and reporting that surfaces the “so what”. My portfolio spans business and skills support, youth and community initiatives using trauma-informed practice, and environmental/heritage programmes. I manage clients and multi-partner fieldwork, facilitate workshops, and translate evidence into concise recommendations and KPIs for delivery teams and funders. Internally, I’ve strengthened quality and pace by establishing a Visuals Hub and reusable report templates, mentoring colleagues, and contributing to bids and onboarding. The common thread is practical evidence that improves decisions, delivery and user experience.
